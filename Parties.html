Bubble and Squeak Parties

We offer three different party packages, depending on the age of your child.

  1. Bubble and Squeak Teddy Bears Picnic – ages 2–6
  2. Bubble and Squeak Pizza Party – Ages 5-12
  3. Bubble and Squeak Mad Hatter’s Tea Party – Ages 8-15
Party Package 1

Teddy Bear’s Picnic

2 hours, Recommended for ages 2-6 years

Children make:

  • Little teddy sandwiches – cheese, ham, jam, cucumber
  • Decorate gummy Bear cupcakes or teddy bear biscuits

We can also provide:

  • Cocktail sausages
  • Carrot and cucumber sticks
  • Crisps
  • Fruit
  • Party bags
