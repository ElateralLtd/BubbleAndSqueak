Parties

We offer three different party packages, depending on the age of your child.

  1. Bubble and Squeak Teddy Bears Picnic – ages 2–6
  2. Bubble and Squeak Pizza Party – Ages 5+
  3. Bubble and Squeak Mad Hatter’s Tea Party – Ages 8+

We can also provide ‘Bake your own cookies’ party bags at £2 each

Teddy Bear’s Picnic

1.5 hours, Recommended for ages 2-5 years

Activity 1 - Children decorate teddy bear biscuits with icing and sprinkles

Activity 2 - Choose one of the following activities:

  • Cupcake decorating
  • Shaped sandwich making
  • Fruity caterpillar kebabs
  • Fondant icing bugs
  • Banoffee pots
  • Fruity yoghurt pots

Once the children have completed the activities, they can then enjoy eating them and sing happy birthday.

To finish, children decorate a box to take their biscuits orcakes home in.

NB. Although support is always given from the party leader,parents may need to support younger children in decorating and creating their food items.

£135 + £4.00 per child

Pizza Party

1.5 hours, Recommended for ages 5-12 years

Activity 1 - Children create their own pizza from scratch andcan choose from a range of toppings.

Activity 2 - Whilst the pizzas are baking, the children decoratea pizza box and can play pizza based games.

Once the pizzas are cooked, the children can enjoy eating theirpizza with their friends and finish off their meal with a pre-made cookie. Leftovers (if there are any!) can be packed up intheir pizza box to go home.

£135 + £5.00 per child

Cookery Party

1.5 hours, Recommended for ages 5-12 years

Activity 1 - Choose from one of the following for children to bake:

  • Themed cupcakes
  • Themed biscuits

Children eat party food / sing happy birthday, etc (30 mins)

Activity 2 - Once the cupcakes or biscuits are baked, childrendecorate their creations with coloured icing and sprinkles.

To finish, children decorate a box to take their biscuits orcakes home in.

£135 + £5.00 per child

Cookery Party

2 hours, Recommended for ages 5-12 years

Activity 1 - Bigger Bake - Choose from one of the following:

  • Cupcakes (theme of your choice)
  • Biscuits (theme of your choice)
  • Pizza
  • Chocolate pudding
  • Cheese straws
  • Jam tarts
  • Flatbreads
  • Scones
  • Muffins - chocolate/lemon/orange/blueberry
  • Puff pastry tarts
  • Puff pastry pinwheels

Whilst the Bigger Bake is in the oven, children move onto their next activity.

Activity 2 - Something Simple - Choose from one of the following:

  • Fondant icing creatures
  • Marshmallow lollipops
  • Fruit kebabs
  • Shaped sandwiches
  • Rainbow cookie dough
  • Fruit salad
  • Banoffee pots
  • Cupcake / biscuit decorating
  • Fruity yoghurt pots

Once both the activities are complete, the children can then enjoy their food or box it up to take home. We provide cake boxes/pizza boxes for the children to decorate and take their food home in.

£150 + £6 per child

Afternoon Tea Party

Activity 1 -- Bigger Bake -Choose from one of the following:
